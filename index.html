How does it work?

  • Connecting Students and Alumni

    Aluma provides a seamless platform where students can connect with experienced alumni from their own institute. Students can search for alumni by profession, expertise, or industry, and initiate valuable conversations through direct messaging, webinars, and discussion forums. This connection gives students access to personalized mentorship, career guidance, and networking opportunities.

  • Exclusive Webinars and Events

    The platform hosts informative webinars, workshops, and events conducted by trusted alumni. These sessions offer reliable insights into industry trends, career advice, and academic guidance. Students can attend these events based on their interests and even participate in interactive Q&A sessions to enhance their understanding.?

  • Institute Support and Alumni Engagement

    Aluma works as a dynamic tool for institutes to foster a sense of community by keeping alumni engaged with their alma mater. Alumni can offer valuable support by contributing their time and expertise through mentorship or webinars. In return, the institute benefits from creating a robust alumni-student network that enhances its overall reputation and impact on student success.
